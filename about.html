About Me

As a native of Virginia, I embarked on a journey of academic exploration that led me from Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU) to Kennesaw State University (KSU), where I've found my niche in interactive design. This transition wasn't merely about changing schools; it represented a pivotal moment in my pursuit of blending creativity with practicality. From the landscapes of Virginia to the dynamic academic environment of KSU, each step has shaped my identity and fueled my passion for visual storytelling.

My affinity for art and design has been a constant thread throughout my life, guiding me towards a path that allows me to express myself creatively while honing my technical skills. Discovering the field of interactive design was like finding the missing puzzle piece; it seamlessly merged my love for aesthetics with the demands of modern design practices. Whether it's crafting digital illustrations in Adobe Illustrator or meticulously designing interfaces in Figma, I find immense joy in bringing ideas to life through visual mediums.

Looking ahead, my goals in interactive design are as multifaceted as the field itself. I aspire to not only master the tools and techniques of the trade but also to make a meaningful impact through my work. Whether it's creating user-friendly interfaces that enhance digital experiences or leveraging design to address social issues, I'm driven by a desire to innovate and inspire. Rooted in my Virginia upbringing and nurtured by the academic community at KSU, I'm excited to continue my journey of exploration and growth in the ever-evolving world of interactive design.
